      Caramelized Rice Cereal Bars

      Par Amelia Feuss.

      Publi√© le 15 July 2025

      Snack bars made with rice cereal and caramel. Uses honey and sunflower seeds. Sweet and crunchy. Good for an afternoon snack. No gluten or dairy.
      Prep: 15 min
      Cook: 20 min
      Total: 35 min
      Servings: 16 servings
      #snack #gluten-free #easy recipe #sweet #bars
      Rice cereal bars. Crunchy yet soft. Sweet from honey. Puffed rice adds lightness. Sunflower seeds give a nutty bite. Melted chocolate for richness. Quick to make. Easy to store. Perfect for snack time. Versatile too. Can add dried fruits or spices. Fun for kids to help.

      Ingr√©dients

      For the base

      • 200g puffed rice cereal
      • 120ml honey
      • 55g sunflower seeds
      • 45g coconut oil
      • 30g dark chocolate, chopped

      √Ä propos des ingr√©dients

      Measurements adjusted for ease. Puffed rice cereal is the base. Replace nuts easily with seeds if needed. Chocolate optional. Use any desired sweetness. Honey coats well. Coconut oil makes it stick. Adjust sweetness based on preference.

      Instructions

      For the base

      1. Melt coconut oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
      2. Stir in honey until it starts bubbling.
      3. Remove from heat. Mix in chocolate and seeds.
      4. In a large bowl, combine puffed rice cereal.
      5. Pour the melted mixture over the cereal. Stir until evenly coated.

        6. For assembly

        1. Line a baking dish with parchment paper.
        2. Transfer the mixture into the dish. Press down firmly.
        3. Let cool at room temperature for 20 minutes.
        4. Once set, cut into bars.

      Conseils de cuisson

      Melt coconut oil first. Combine with sweetener. Mix in chocolate for smoothness. Stir in puffed rice last. Press firmly to create bars. Cooling is essential for setting. Store in airtight container. Enjoy within a week for best freshness.
