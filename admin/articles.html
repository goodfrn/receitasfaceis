Delicious Cherry Almond Maple Crisp Recipe

Welcome to my kitchen, where the magic of baking truly comes alive! Today, I'm thrilled to share a delightful recipe for Cherry Almond Maple Crisp.

Ingredients

  • 980 grams frozen cherries
  • 60 millilitres maple syrup

About the Ingredients

The beauty of this cherry almond maple crisp lies in its simplicity, made from wholesome ingredients you probably already have in your pantry.

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine frozen cherries, maple syrup, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  2. Transfer the cherry filling to a baking dish; a nine-inch square pan works great.

Cooking Tips

To get the most out of this delicious cherry almond maple crisp, make sure to toss your cherries gently but thoroughly.

Tips & Advice

  • This cherry almond maple crisp is highly customizable! Feel free to swap frozen cherries for other fruits.
